## ЁЯОи Design Guidelines (Apply to ALL Slides)

### Color Palette (Based on MaCare Brand)
- **Primary**: Pink/Rose (#EC4899, #F43F5E) - Maternal care
- **Secondary**: Blue/Cyan (#3B82F6, #06B6D4) - Healthcare professionals
- **Accent**: Green (#10B981) - Health/Success
- **Alert**: Orange/Red (#F97316, #EF4444) - Urgency
- **Background**: White/Light gradient (#F9FAFB to #FEF2F2)

### Typography
- **Title Font**: Poppins/Montserrat Bold (40-48pt)
- **Body Font**: Poppins/Montserrat Regular (18-24pt)
- **Accent**: Poppins/Montserrat SemiBold

### Layout Consistency
- Logo top-left on every slide
- Slide number bottom-right
- Consistent margins (60px all sides)
- Use icons and minimal text
- High-quality images/illustrations

A: 2-day training program for midwives, 4-hour webinar for doctors. In-app tutorials and ongoing support. Partnership with nursing colleges.

A: Only Bengali-first platform, role separation (doctor/midwife), government integration ready, free tier always available, culturally appropriate content.

**Q5: How do you ensure data accuracy?**
A: Midwife-verified data entry, doctor review for critical metrics, automated validation rules, regular quality audits, feedback loops.

**Q6: What about user adoption/acceptance?**
A: Pilot includes community engagement, health worker ambassadors, success stories sharing. Free tier removes financial barrier. Bengali interface removes language barrier.

**Q7: Revenue timeline to profitability?**
A: Break-even at 10,000 premium users (achievable Month 18). Profitable by Year 2 with diversified revenue. Pilot phase primarily grant/investment funded.

**Q8: How do you handle medical liability?**
A: Platform is advisory, not diagnostic. Licensed medical professionals responsible for advice. Clear terms of service. Professional liability insurance for providers.
